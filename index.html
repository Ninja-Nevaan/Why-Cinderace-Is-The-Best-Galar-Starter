Why Cinderace Is The Best Galar Starter Explained

The reason Cinderace is the best Galar starter, is because of it's speed, speed defense, it is a good kicker, and mainly because it's ability Libero! This ability lets Cinderace change it's type to the type of move it uses. For example, if Cinderace is up against a water type, it can use Electro Ball to get the advatage. Or, if it is up against a rock type, it can use Iron Head to gain the upper hand. That sort of Pokémon combined with a good moveset would equal a really OP Pokémon!
